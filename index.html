Below is Marcus Aurelius, he was a Roman Emperor who ruled from 161 AD until his death in 180 AD. Marcus Aurelius is known for his philosophical writings, particularly his book "Meditations," which he wrote in Greek during his military campaigns. The book is a collection of personal reflections and stoic philosophy, and is considered one of the greatest works of philosophy in the Western tradition.
